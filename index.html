My Story

I am a go-getter Software Engineer with a background in Health Science and Business.

From the human body to web applications, I am a systems nerd who loves analyzing patterns to find solutions.

This passion lead me to study Health Science with a concentration in Physical Therapy at Boston University where I blended my interests in human anatomy and problem solving.

Armed with the knowledge of the human body and how to rehab it, I co-founded REVAMP Training LLC, a private personal training business.

At REVAMP, I developed the business and marketing systems and helped over 100 clients from adolescents to those with Parkinson's reach their fitness goals.

After seeing the disparity of access to wellness for many minorities, I transitioned to tech to widen my impact and help more people.
