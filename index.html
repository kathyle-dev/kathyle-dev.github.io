MY STORY

From the human body to web applications, I am a systems nerd who loves analyzing patterns and problems to find solutions.

I was born in Boston to Chinese-Vietnamese immigrants. As the child of parents who didn't speak English, I held multiple hats at home: the "translator," "google", and my favorite, the "anything-electronic-fixer." I grew up being thrown into the fire, having to figure things out on the fly and using my resources to find solutions to any obstacles my family had. I attended Boston Latin School and then Boston University where I blended my interests in human anatomy and problem solving by focusing my studies in Health Science with a concentration in Physical Therapy. Armed with the knowledge of the human body and how to rehab it, I helped over 100 clients from adolescents to those with Parkinson's improve their functional movements and reach their fitness goals. After seeing the disparity of access to health and wellness for many minorities, I transitioned to tech in hopes of widening my impact and to help more people.
